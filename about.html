What is Engineering Toolbox?

Engineering Toolbox is a free collection of industrial engineering calculators designed for manufacturing engineers, production planners, supply chain professionals, and quality engineers. Every tool on this site is built around standard, peer-reviewed engineering formulas.

The goal is simple: give engineers fast, accurate, browser-based tools they can use on the shop floor, in a meeting, or at their desk — without paying for software, waiting for Excel to load, or digging through textbooks for formulas.

Formula Sources & Standards

Every calculator is based on internationally recognised engineering standards and academic references:

  • OEE — ISO 22400-2, SEMI E10, VDMA 66412
  • Cp / Cpk — AIAG SPC Manual (4th ed.), ISO 21747
  • Takt Time / Cycle Time — Lean manufacturing principles (Toyota Production System)
  • EOQ / Safety Stock / ROP — APICS CPIM Body of Knowledge, Wilson EOQ model
  • PERT — PMI PMBOK Guide, US Navy 1957 original methodology
  • MCDM / Weighted Scoring — Simple Additive Weighting (SAW), peer-reviewed MCDM literature
  • Break-Even — Standard managerial accounting (cost-volume-profit analysis)
