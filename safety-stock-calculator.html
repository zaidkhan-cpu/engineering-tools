What is Safety Stock?

Safety stock is an extra quantity of inventory held to protect against unexpected increases in demand or delays in supplier delivery. Without safety stock, any variation from your average demand or lead time would cause a stockout — lost sales, production stoppages, or unhappy customers.

The Safety Stock Formula

The standard formula using demand variability only:

Safety Stock = Z × σdemand × √Lead Time

The advanced formula accounting for both demand and lead time variability:

Safety Stock = Z × √(LT × σd² + d² × σLT²)

Safety Stock vs Reorder Point

  • Safety stock — the buffer quantity you always keep on hand above your cycle stock
  • Reorder point — the inventory level that triggers a new order: ROP = (avg demand × lead time) + safety stock
  • Cycle stock — the inventory that gets consumed between orders (related to EOQ)
